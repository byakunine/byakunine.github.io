Who am I

I am a graduate student undertaking Information Systems Management at Carneige Mellon Univeristy, pursuing a career in the data science field. I worked as a tax technology analyst at Ernst & Young for the past 3 years, self-taught C#, SQL, and VBA through projects at work. My Bachelor of Applied Mathematics degree at UCLA equipped me with strong problem solving and logic building skills. My plan in the upcoming 5 years is to become a proactive and fast learning data scientist and to work on practical analytical projects with my methodical skills and relevant expertise to achieve goals.
